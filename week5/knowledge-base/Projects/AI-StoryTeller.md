# AI-StoryTeller ✍️🤖

AI-StoryTeller is a Streamlit-powered application that helps users create, enhance, and polish engaging stories using Google Gemini 1.5 Flash. The tool guides users through three creative tiers: drafting, structural enhancement, and artistic polishing — making it a complete storytelling assistant.

## 🎯 Key Features
### 📝 TIER 1: Story Drafting

* Choose story origin (personal or adapted tale).

* Customize use case, focus traits, time frame, and story type.

* Optional guided storytelling framework (emotion, conflict, growth, etc.).

* Generates your first story draft with Gemini AI.

### 🚀 TIER 2: Story Enhancement

* Offers structural storytelling methods like:

* Hero’s Journey

* The Story Spine

* Cliffhanger

* In Media Res, and more

* Option to book a 1:1 live coaching session or auto-enhance with AI.

* Saves enhanced story for final polishing.

### 🎨 TIER 3: Story Polishing

* Add creative elements:

* Impactful quotes/poems

* Similes & comparisons

* Creative enhancements

* Emotional resonance tips

* Save and download your final polished draft.

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

* Frontend/UI: Streamlit

* Gemini 1.5 Flash
